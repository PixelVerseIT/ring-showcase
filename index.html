Blurb

Introducing The Triangle Ring – a striking and modern piece of jewelry inspired by geometric shapes, particularly the power and balance of the triangle. This design reflects simplicity and strength, making it perfect for those who appreciate minimalist yet bold fashion. The ring features a sleek black band with a prominent gold triangle on top, offering a clean, sharp look.

For this project, I selected stainless steel for the band on Shapeways due to its recyclable and durable nature, making it an eco-friendly choice. The triangle, in gold-plated brass, adds a touch of elegance while also being a sustainable option. By choosing these materials, the ring not only looks great but also aligns with environmentally responsible practices by reducing material waste and supporting recycling.

This ring is aimed at teenagers and young adults who have a love for modern, edgy style. With its minimalist appeal and sustainable materials, it’s perfect for individuals who want to express their unique style while being conscious of the environment.
