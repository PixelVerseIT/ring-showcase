About the Project

This modern ring design project is a creative exploration undertaken as part of my Year 8 design tech class. The goal was to push the boundaries of conventional jewelry design, creating a unique and contemporary ring that seamlessly blends aesthetics with functionality.

Throughout this journey, I've honed my skills in various areas:

  • Design thinking and conceptualization
  • Sketching and visual communication
  • 3D modeling and digital rendering
  • Understanding of jewelry design principles
  • Awareness of sustainable practices in manufacturing

The final design features a twisted band that symbolizes life's intricate journey, complemented by an ethically sourced diamond as the centerpiece. By incorporating recycled gold, this piece not only captivates with its beauty but also aligns with sustainable jewelry practices, embodying a harmonious blend of aesthetics and responsibility.
